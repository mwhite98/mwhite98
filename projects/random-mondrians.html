Mondrian Art Generator

This project was for a Functional and Logic Programming course in 2018. Based off of Dr. Ben Stephenson’s Mondrian Art project, my partner and I used Haskell to implement a recursive algorithm that output Mondrian style art. Colours, box sizes, and box shapes were randomly generated to create the artwork, and fit a theme as depicted by the user. The user personalized their artwork through sharing their own character traits.
